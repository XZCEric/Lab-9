Discoveries of TOI-1452b

The exoplanet was discovered by a team from the University of Montreal, but the planet is in an excellent position for further examination by the new James Webb Space Telescope.

TOI-1452b's general information

  • 100 light year away
  • 4.8 times massive than earth
  • 10% to 25% covered by water

Is this Planet Habitable

Goldilocks Zone

The 'Goldilocks Zone,' or habitable zone, is the range of distance with the right temperatures for water to remain liquid.

Covered by Water

Due to its lower density compared to Earth, scientists think it may be covered by a thick layer of water

Too Far Away

Sadly planet TOI-1452b is 100 light years away from us, with our current technology it will take about 2 million years.
